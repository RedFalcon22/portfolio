Who I Am

A developer who competes at everything he does.

I'm Khalil, a Computer Science student at ISTIC and a passionate Full Stack Developer. My focus is on building secure, scalable, and user-friendly digital solutions — from intuitive mobile apps to intelligent AI platforms.

My technical range covers Flutter, Django, Python, Angular, Java EE, and increasingly AI/ML. My PFE internship project — a cyberbullying detection system built with RAG and transformer models — reflects a conviction that the best engineering solves real human problems.

Before writing code full-time, I spent 14 years as an elite basketball player, representing Tunisia internationally. I also play piano, shoot photography, and have traveled through four continents. That breadth isn't incidental — it's how I think.
